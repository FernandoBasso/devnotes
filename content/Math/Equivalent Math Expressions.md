## Half

$$
\frac{1}{2}n = \frac{n}{2}
$$
Multiplying a value by one-half (0.5) is t he same as dividing the value by 2. 0.5 is 50%, and 50% is half something. Dividing a value by two halves the value.

And these are also equivalent:

- $\frac{1}{3}n = \frac{n}{3}$
- $\frac{1}{8}n = \frac{n}{8}$
