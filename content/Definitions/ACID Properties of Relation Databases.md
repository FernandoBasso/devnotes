## Intro

ACID stands for Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation and Durability.

### Atomicity

Transactions are “all or nothing”.

### Consistency

After a transaction (either a successful or a failed one), the data in the database is in a consistent state.

### Isolation

Transactions are independent.

### Durability

After transactions are committed, the data is there to stay.
