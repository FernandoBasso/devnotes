## App is damaged macOS pop-up

On macOS, it often happens that upon attempting to open Xournal++, a pop-up shows up saying that the app is damaged and must be moved to trash.

```text
$ xattr -c /Applications/Xournal++.app
```

- https://github.com/xournalpp/xournalpp/issues/6305
